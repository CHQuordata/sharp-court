The 5/09 patch set fixed bugs inside the AI ANALYZE pipeline. This patch set is a philosophy shift in how every pick is sized and consumed. Pipeline shape mostly unchanged; what's added are new filters at the front, new badges on the leaderboard, and a fundamentally honest stake calculation.

FIX #12 — TENNIS SPREAD CAPS
Cap HIGH on wide spreads + plus-money spreads
Backtest finding: spread CLV is +3.08pp (right side, right price) but W/L is 10-15. Side selection sound; aggressive line + plus-money is the leak. Cap HIGH on -4g+ spreads (0-3 historical) and plus-money spreads (book pricing implies hard-to-clear line). Filter tag: spread_size_capped.

FIX #14 — POST-UPSET LETDOWN DETECTION
Flag players coming off recent upset wins
Real tennis pattern: emotional/physical comedown after upsetting a higher-ranked opponent. Detection: if player's most recent match (within 5 days) was a W vs opponent ranked ≥10 spots higher, the prompt context emits a ⚠️ POST-UPSET LETDOWN tag. System prompt rule: post-upset favorites get faded or capped at MEDIUM; post-upset dogs cannot be elevated by the upset itself ("upset isn't repeatable form"). Backtest hits 20% of historical picks (16/81); preferentially catches HIGH plus-money longshot losses.
