Symptom: TMW silently dropped 8 of 20 games whose ESPN start times bucketed into "May 11" local; live matches that crossed midnight vanished from TODAY

  ref.setDate(ref.getDate()+1);

AFTER — rolling window + live rescue

const now=new Date();

const dayAfter=new Date(
  now.getTime()+48*3600e3);

  g=g.filter(x=>
    x.tm.toDateString()===todayStr
    || x.st==='STATUS_IN_PROGRESS');

    x.tm.toDateString()!==todayStr
    && x.tm>now && x.tm<=dayAfter);

▎ NET IMPACT
